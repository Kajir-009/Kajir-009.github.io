## What is this

Hunters: Awakening is a 2D JRPG I made in Unity during my second year of University. It's the first installment of the Hunters IP, 
conceived as an expandable universe that will enable the development of multiple games with different protagonists, stories, and genres, 
all connected by the conflict between dimensions and the role of the hunters.

## Overview

Hunters: Awakening is a 2D JRPG game inspired by Chilean mythology, blending supernatural storytelling, exploration, and strategic combat in a world where the ordinary and the hidden collide.
The player takes on the role of Javiera, a Chilean teenager who travels to the remote Chirrica Island to visit her family. 
What begins as a peaceful stay quickly turns into an unsettling experience: relentless rain, sightings of unknown creatures, and a series of 
unexplained murders begin to disrupt the island’s apparent normality. Everything changes one night when Javiera is attacked by small goblin-like 
creatures—descendants of the Trauco—and is rescued by a mysterious individual: Brook, a hunter belonging to the organization known as Hunters. 

### Storyline

The Hunters organization is responsible for preserving the balance between dimensions, confronting entities that threaten to break into the human world. These creatures, invisible to most, can only be perceived by hunters or individuals with special abilities. 
After her encounter with Brook, Javiera discovers that she possesses a unique sensitivity to these entities. Forced by circumstance, she must join the Hunters to protect her reality and uncover the origin of the phenomena affecting the island. 
As the story unfolds, what initially seemed like an isolated case begins to reveal a much greater threat.
